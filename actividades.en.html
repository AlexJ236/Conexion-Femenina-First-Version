Halloween Picnic

On Halloween, we organized a very special picnic with our She Speaks English program interns and some volunteers. It was a day full of fun, face masks and, most importantly, a space to share American culture. During the picnic, we read together a witch story in English, which allowed us to see the progress of our fellows and their growth in the language. It was a perfect opportunity to enjoy such a symbolic celebration while we reinforced the learning acquired in the program. This event not only celebrated Halloween, but also the effort and dedication of all our participants, creating a warm, educational and joyful atmosphere.
