Our Campaigns

A Hug Against the Cold

"A Hug Against the Cold" is more than a campaign. It is a response to the needs of so many people who, on the coldest days, struggle to find a little warmth, not only in their bodies, but also in their hearts. In this campaign, we come together to bring shelter to those who need it most: mothers, fathers and children who, many times, have to face the cold without the slightest protection. Through donations of clothes, blankets and food, we seek to give them more than a physical coat; we want to give them hope, closeness and a reminder that they are not alone. What began as a small initiative has become a movement full of love and solidarity. With each donated garment, with each gesture of support, we seek to send a clear message: together we can make a difference. Because the cold is not only a condition of the weather, but also of the hearts that have not yet found a hug. And with every action we take, we are sending them that warm hug, full of support, so that they know that no matter how far they are, they will always have someone to take care of them, to see them and who cares about their well-being. Your help can be the hug they need most. With your collaboration, we can continue to bring a little warmth to more homes, to more people who only expect to be seen and treated with dignity.

Christmas Campaigns (Chincha & Ica)

Every Christmas, what we want most is to make people feel special and remind them that love, solidarity and hope are alive. The Women's Connection Christmas Campaigns are born with the idea of bringing joy and affection to those who need it most, especially to the most vulnerable families, who may not have what it takes to celebrate. Through these campaigns, we collect donations of food, toys, clothes and everything that can make Christmas a warmer moment, full of smiles and, above all, hope. We want no girl or boy to be left without a toy, that every family feels that there is a ray of light, and that no woman feels forgotten during these holidays. But it's not just about material gifts. Each delivery is a hug, a gesture of support that says "we see you, we love you, you are not alone." Christmas is not just a date; it is a reminder that union, love and compassion are the greatest gifts we can give. Join us on this mission. With your support, we can bring smiles to more children, more families and more hearts who need to remember that Christmas is synonymous with love and hope, beyond the material. Because a simple gesture can transform a life.

Close Cycles and Open Smiles

"Close cycles, open smiles" is a campaign that is born from love and resilience. We know that closing a cycle is not always easy, but we believe in the power of transforming pain into hope. We invite brave women to donate those stuffed animals that were once part of a story that no longer hurts, to turn them into Christmas gifts full of illusion for girls and boys who dream of a detail that gives them back joy. Each donated stuffed animal carries an act of liberation... and is transformed into a new smile. Because when we let go with love, we also give love.
