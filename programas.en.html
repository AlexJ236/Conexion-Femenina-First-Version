Our Programs

She Speaks English

“She Speaks English” is a scholarship program driven by Conexión Femenina that provides free English classes to young women in the province of Chincha. We believe that language can be a powerful tool to open doors, broaden horizons, and strengthen confidence. Here, each scholarship represents a story of hope. Each class is a step towards confidence, leadership, and the freedom to choose one's own path. Many of our participants are talented, strong young women with a great desire to get ahead, but without the resources to access quality education. This program offers them what they deserve: a real opportunity. “She Speaks English” does not have large companies or million-dollar budgets behind it. It has a giant heart, a committed team, and a community that believes in the power of women. So far, we have come far with very little. But we dream of going even further. You too can be part of this dream. With your support — large or small — you help us continue to change stories, one word, one class, one voice at a time.

Entre Nosotras

“Entre Nosotras” (Between Us) was born from the urgent need to create a safe, human, and deeply empathetic space among women. A place where we can speak without fear, heal without shame, and grow without feeling alone. Because we know that many times, what we need most is not an immediate solution, but someone to listen to us, to understand us, and to say: “I went through that too.” This program offers emotional workshops, talks on self-care, self-love, healthy relationships, and violence prevention, led by committed professionals and women who have personally experienced what they now share with love and sisterhood. Here there are no judgments. Only empathy. Here there is no competition. Only support. Here there are no silences. Only voices that accompany each other. “Entre Nosotras” is that hug that many of us did not receive in time. It is a network woven with stories, tears, laughter, and courage. A space that has transformed the lives of many women in Chincha who, for years, believed they were alone. And they are not. You too can be part of this network. Your support, your voice, or your gesture can help more women find in this space what they have been looking for so long: understanding, strength, and new possibilities.

Conexión sin Barreras

“Conexión Sin Barreras” (Connection Without Barriers) is an act of love, empathy, and dignity. It is a program that seeks to include women with disabilities in daily life, in spaces where for years they have been invisible, marginalized, or underestimated. This project was born from the strength and real story of a woman with a disability who, despite everything, managed to get ahead. Today, that same woman becomes a guide, a friend, a role model. She visits other women with disabilities, listens to them, embraces them with her story, and reminds them that they are not alone, that their lives matter, and that they also have the right to dream, laugh, grow, and belong. “Conexión Sin Barreras” is much more than a social program. It is a fraternal network built visit by visit, word by word, from real experience and the will to transform realities. We want every woman to know that her voice matters, that her existence is necessary, and that she can believe in herself again, even on the most difficult days. Here we do not come to save anyone. We come to walk together. To break invisible barriers. To make visible what was always valuable. With your support, we can reach more homes, more hearts, more women who hope to be seen not for their limitations, but for their immense worth.

Promo que Inspira

We all dream of that special moment: prom night. A beautiful dress, music, photos with friends, and the feeling of having made it this far. But for many young women, that dream seems distant, almost impossible... not for lack of desire, but for lack of resources. “Promo que Inspira” (Prom that Inspires) was born to accompany them, to tell them that yes, they can, and that they are not alone. This program guides girls who want to celebrate their prom party but cannot afford it. I teach them — as a mentor and from experience — to organize themselves, to undertake, to seek funds with creativity, effort, and heart. I help them believe in themselves, trust their abilities, and transform a dream into an achievable goal. Because here it is not just about a party. It is about learning to fight for what one wants. To feel capable, valuable, and worthy of celebrating their story. I have seen how their eyes shine when they manage to sell their first product, when they see that together they move forward, when they understand that what seemed unattainable becomes real. Promo que Inspira is that: a hug, a guide, a helping hand. And you too can be part of it. With your support, we can continue to help more girls celebrate not only their prom but everything they have overcome to get there.
