Our Mission

To empower the women of Chincha through education, personal development, psychological support, and access to opportunities that allow them to reach their full potential. We believe in the power of connection, unity, and collaboration to build a more just and equitable society.

Our Vision

To be a benchmark for change in the lives of vulnerable women, promoting their integral development and transforming them into agents of change within their communities. We aspire to expand our work to other regions, bringing our message of hope and empowerment to more women in Peru.
